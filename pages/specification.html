RTX 3090 Ti

The GeForce RTX 3090 Ti is an enthusiast-class graphics card by NVIDIA,
launched on January 27th, 2022. Built on the 8 nm process, and based on the
GA102 graphics processor, in its GA102-350-A1 variant, the card
supports DirectX 12 Ultimate. This ensures that all modern games
will run on GeForce RTX 3090 Ti.
